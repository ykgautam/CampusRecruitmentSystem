OUR STUDENT COMMUNITIES

Debating Society

The society collectively trains and participates in parliamentary debates around the country and adds to the trans-discipliniary experience of its members.

ECA Societies

Apart from academic prowess, our students are well versed in the fields of Art and Culture. We house various societies for Choreography, Western & Classical Dance, Fine Arts, Singing, Photography etc.

Entrepreneurial-Cell

It is an initiative to ignite the entrepreneurial spirit amongst the youth and create a transition from classroom to workplace. It organises workshops, panel discussions etc.

TEDx

The event aims at exploring ideas that can serve as a catalyst to larger change. Sessions are held with dignitaries from all walks of life who share their experience and wisdom.

Department Societies

Every department works in its own sphere to heighten intellectual stimulation amongst its students. Departments actively organise seminars, panel discussions, quizzes, competitions etc.

Finance and Investment Cell

The society aims at providing a common platform to finance and investment enthusiasts by conducting discussions, seminars, competitions and stressing on practical application.

Samagam (Youth conference)

The annual event aims at propagating an exchange of ideas across generations by organising speaker sessions with some of the most eminent names in today's society.

NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit of the college works under Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, with the aim of inculcating the spirit of service among college students to help individuals and communities cope up with their problems

Pixels

The photography society of the college works with the aim of exploring the photographer within ourselves. It's a grand platform which aims to put together all the Mobilegraphers, Semi-DSLR users and enthusiasts to shape up their skills of a photographer.

MarkUs

The marketing society works with the vision of enhancing functional and contemporary knowledge of marketing within the students.

SPIC MACAY

SPIC MACAY is a non political nationwide voluntary movement that organised programmes in schools and colleges to make students aware about Indian and World heritage.

CULINARY ARTS SOCIETY

The objective of Culinary Arts Society is to vitrine the spectacular assortment of food stretching from Indian to various world-class cuisines.

Kalakriti

The fine arts society, is a step towards promotion of art and creativity in the college. It is a platform for the students to bring out their creativity and to enhance their knowledge about different forms of creative art.

Illuminati

Established in 2007, is the quizzing society of a Hansraj College. Constituted by enthusiastic quizzers. lluminati aims to bring together quizzers from variety of courses in the college, to hone their skills and dispel the myths surrounding quizzing.

Haritima

Haritima is the environmental society of the college which works with the motto of spreading environmental awareness amongst the students through cycle rallies and cleanliness campaigns.

Equinox

Equinox is an eloquent speaker session which brings together professionals from myriad fields and backgrounds to share their valuable experiences, their indomitable spirit, and struggles which ultimately led them on the road to success.
